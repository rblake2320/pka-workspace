Unc & Neph Vibe Coding started as more than just writing code. It became a way to learn faster, build smarter, and create things that people actually find useful.

The uncle-and-nephew dynamic is part of the foundation: one side brings experience, systems thinking, and vision; the other brings energy, curiosity, and fresh perspective. Together, that creates a brand built around experimentation, growth, and real-world creation.

We're building skills, products, and opportunities in public — turning ideas into websites, tools, automations, and digital experiences that can grow into something bigger.

This is a lab, not an agency. A studio, not a portfolio. A workshop that keeps the lights on by shipping things people want to keep using — and posting the receipts when we do.

Some of what we make will be services. Some will be products. Some will be experiments that teach us something we didn't know we needed to know. All of it is part of the same compounding curve.

03 — Why "Vibe Coding"

It's creative velocity,
not random messing around.

The phrase is cool but it needs framing. Vibe coding is our shorthand for creative, high-momentum building — where ideas move quickly into prototypes, experiments, and shipped products. AI-assisted, opinionated, and intentional.

What it's not
  • Random coding
  • Unserious work
  • Low-quality experiments
  • Vibes without output
  • Demo-ware
What it is
  • Creative velocity
  • Modern product building
  • AI-assisted development
  • Experimentation with intent
  • Shipping useful things, quickly
04 — What We Believe

Five beliefs the lab runs on.

v.01

Build with purpose.

Every build should answer a real question for a real person.

v.02

Learn fast.

Each shipped thing teaches the next thing. Compound interest.

v.03

Ship often.

Imperfect and public beats perfect and private. Always.

v.04

Stay useful.

If no one misses it when it's gone, it didn't earn its spot.

v.05

Keep evolving.

Tools change. Models change. The taste for shipping doesn't.
