# Unc & Neph Vibe Coding — Full Website Blueprint

## 1) Core Brand Positioning

**Brand name:** Unc & Neph Vibe Coding  
**Meaning:** Uncle + Nephew building, learning, shipping, and turning ideas into real products.  
**Big idea:** A creative build lab where family, experimentation, AI, automation, software, and entrepreneurship meet.

### Brand statement
We build in public, sharpen our coding instincts, turn ideas into working products, and grow real skills into marketable solutions.

### One-line positioning
A family-powered AI and software build lab creating useful tools, experiments, and digital products.

### What makes it different
- Family dynamic gives the brand personality and authenticity.
- The site is not just a portfolio. It is a living build lab.
- It shows growth, experimentation, shipping, and real-world usefulness.
- It can grow into services, products, community, education, and content.

### Tone
- Smart
- Creative
- Clean
- Confident
- Human
- Builder energy
- Experimental, but not sloppy

### Brand personality
Think: part innovation studio, part product lab, part public workshop.

## 4) Site Strategy

The site should do **four jobs**:

1. **Tell the story** — who Unc & Neph are and why this exists.
2. **Show the work** — projects, prototypes, experiments, tools.
3. **Offer value** — services, products, templates, automations, MVP help.
4. **Build a following** — content, updates, build logs, mailing list, community later.

This should feel like a **hybrid of a creative studio, startup lab, and build-in-public brand**.

## 14) Best Visual Identity Direction

### Style recommendation
Use a **modern creative-tech aesthetic**.

### Look and feel
- Clean typography
- Bold headlines
- Spacious layout
- Minimal but sharp interactions
- Slight glow, grid, or blueprint-inspired accents
- Tech-lab feel without cliché hacker visuals

### Color direction options

#### Option A: Dark modern
- Background: near-black / deep charcoal
- Accent: electric blue or neon cyan
- Secondary accent: warm gold or muted purple
- Text: off-white

#### Option B: Clean premium light
- Background: soft white / light gray
- Accent: deep navy or cobalt
- Secondary accent: lime or orange micro-accent
- Text: rich charcoal

#### Best recommendation
Start with **dark modern** for a stronger brand identity and “lab” feel.

### Typography direction
- Headline font: strong geometric sans
- Body font: clean modern sans

Examples:
- Space Grotesk + Inter
- Sora + Inter
- Plus Jakarta Sans + IBM Plex Sans
